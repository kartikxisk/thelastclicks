Our story

Built on the discipline of premium brands.

We started as a wedding film crew that refused to compromise on craft. Eight years in, we're a studio trusted by Fortune 500 marketing teams, automotive houses, and the most demanding creative directors in the country.

Working with premium and regulated brands has shaped our discipline — lighting, framing, post — and that discipline now powers everything we do, from a single product still to a 12-city brand campaign.

We don't think of ourselves as a vendor. We're a long-term partner that scales with your story.

The journey

Eight years, one obsession.

From a single camera and a borrowed lens to a 60-person studio shooting across 26 cities. Every milestone was earned by saying no to briefs that didn't fit the standard.

2018
Started with one camera.
Founded in Bhopal as a 2-person wedding-film crew. First gig was a 100-guest engagement in our hometown — and we still know the family.
2020
First brand film.
Lockdown forced us to pivot. Within 90 days, our first corporate brand film landed — a regional FMCG launch that's still in their library.
2022
In-house post pipeline.
Stopped outsourcing grade. Built a full DaVinci pipeline with ACES color management. Quality control jumped overnight.
2024
Premium beverage partner.
First Fortune-500 partner. The discipline of working with regulated brands reshaped how we approach every brief.
2026
547+ productions in.
Crew of 60+ across cities. Calendar booked into 2026. Still the same standard, scaled.
Editorial filters

What we don't do.

Saying no clearly is part of the craft. Here's where we walk away.

Spec work for pitches

We don't trade samples for chance. Every film we make goes in our portfolio.

Cheap "social packages"

If craft has to drop to hit a price, we'd rather refer you to someone else.

Outsourced grade

The hand that lit the film grades it. Always in-house, never farmed.

Generic stock libraries

Every frame is shot for your brief. No watermark-removed inserts.

Endless revisions without rationale

Three rounds, written feedback, fast turnaround. Beyond that we re-scope.

Ghost crews

No name swaps mid-project. The team you meet is the team that shoots.
